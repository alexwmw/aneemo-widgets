Reflective Exercise: What Are My Concerns?

What are your main concerns at the moment? Which of them do you have influence and control over? Are you focusing too much on your circle of concern, and not enough on your circle of influence? Try the exercise below. Start to shift the balance back towards your circle of influence.

Take a minute to write down in the box below as many things as you are currently concerned about. This could be within a particular domain – for example, at work or in your career – or they could be more general concerns. Try to be as specific as possible.

When you are finished, if you wish you may click the button at the bottom to save your answers as a pdf.
